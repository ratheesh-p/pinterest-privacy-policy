1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Pinterest Automation Application ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service to automate pin creation on Pinterest.

This App is operated from Germany and primarily serves users in India. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Personal Data Protection Bill.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Pinterest Account Information: Your Pinterest user ID, board information, and pins you create through our App
  • Content Data: Images, titles, descriptions, and links you provide for automated pin creation
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our App, including API requests and automation settings
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and functionality purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the automation service

  • To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Pinterest: We share your content (images, titles, descriptions) with Pinterest through their official API to create pins on your behalf
  • Service Providers: We may use cloud hosting services (such as AWS or GitHub) to store and process data
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Data is stored on servers located in the European Union (Germany) and follows GDPR-compliant practices.

6. Pinterest API Integration

Our App uses the Pinterest API v5 to create pins on your behalf. By using our App, you grant us permission to:

  • Access your Pinterest boards and pin information
  • Create new pins with content you provide
  • Read your board data to facilitate pin creation

You can revoke this access at any time through your Pinterest account settings.
