Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Pinterest Automation Application ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service to automate pin creation on Pinterest.

This App is operated from Bangalore, Karnataka, India and primarily serves users in India. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, relevant Indian data protection rules, and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Pinterest Account Information: Your Pinterest user ID, board information, and pins you create through our App.
  • Content Data: Images, titles, descriptions, and links you provide for automated pin creation.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our App, including API requests and automation settings.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and functionality purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the automation service.
  • To create and publish pins on Pinterest on your behalf.
  • To improve and optimize our App's functionality.
  • To communicate with you about service updates and technical issues.
  • To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Pinterest: We share your content (images, titles, descriptions, and links) with Pinterest through their official API to create pins on your behalf.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted cloud hosting and infrastructure providers to store and process data in a secure manner.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by applicable law or to protect our legal rights and the rights of our users.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard security measures. We use appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We store your Pinterest access tokens and related credentials only to the extent necessary to provide the automation service. Access tokens are stored securely and are used solely to create and manage pins on your behalf through the Pinterest API. You can revoke this access at any time via your Pinterest account settings or by contacting us, after which the tokens will no longer be usable.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Pinterest API Integration

Our App uses the Pinterest API v5 to create pins on your behalf. By using our App, you grant us permission to:

  • Access your Pinterest boards and pin information as permitted by you.
  • Create new pins with the content (images, titles, descriptions, and links) you provide.
  • Read your board data as needed to facilitate pin creation and automation.

You can revoke this access at any time through your Pinterest account settings. Once access is revoked, our App will no longer be able to create new pins or access your Pinterest data.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data, subject to applicable law:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual limitations.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format, where technically feasible.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our App may use cookies or similar technologies to maintain your session and improve user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the App.

9. Children's Privacy
